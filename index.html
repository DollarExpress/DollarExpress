What Is a Personal Loan Marketplace?

A personal loan marketplace allows consumers to submit one simple application and get matched with multiple potential loan providers. Instead of applying separately to many lenders, DollarExpress streamlines the process by evaluating your information and routing it to lenders that may be a good fit.
